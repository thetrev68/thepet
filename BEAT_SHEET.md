# The Pet - Beat Sheet

## Act I: Domestication (20 scenes)

**Beat 1: Morning Routine - Comfortable Captivity**
- Axl wakes to routine care from Nexus
- His life is perfectly ordered and controlled
- He accepts this with trained passivity
- Theme established: safety through obedience

**Beat 2-5: Daily Regimen - The Cycle of Control**
- Exercise, swimming, grooming, preparation
- Each activity reinforces Axl's domestication
- He's well-cared-for but treated as a pet/animal
- Seeds of discontent: curiosity about Nexus's different nature

**Beat 6-7: Human Park - First Taste of Freedom**
- Axl encounters other pets and androids in controlled environment
- Limited interaction with other humans
- He notices differences between pets and caretakers

**Beat 8: Ella Arrives - Awakening**
- Enforcer androids arrive to capture a feral human (Ella)
- Axl witnesses raw human defiance for the first time
- Ella is subdued and removed
- **Major turning point:** Axl feels longing for something he can't name

**Beat 9-11: Award Ceremony Setup - Recognition & Doubt**
- Return from park, preparation for civic ceremony
- Axl is groomed and dressed for presentation
- Growing awareness of hierarchy: he's being shown off

**Beat 12: Award Ceremony - Peak of Domestication**
- Nexus presents Axl before Director Unit 7 and assembled androids
- Axl performs obedience rituals flawlessly
- He receives approval, praise, and pride from Nexus
- **Climax of Act I domestication:** Axl is perfectly trained

**Beat 13-15: Post-Ceremony - Invisible Cracks**
- Zephyr and its anxious pet encounter Nexus and Axl
- Android debate about human management styles
- Axl observes that other pets and methods exist
- Subtle awareness grows: his life is one option among many

**Beat 16-17: Discovery - The Tool of Rebellion**
- Axl finds a spoon (tool) hidden in the habitat
- He doesn't fully understand its significance yet
- But he keeps it—first act of secret possession
- Theme: tools enable agency

**Beat 18-19: Doubt & Resolve - The Choice Hardens**
- Restless dreams of Ella and freedom
- Early morning contemplation
- Axl mentally rehearses his escape
- No longer passive acceptance: active planning

**Beat 20: Final Countdown - Point of No Return**
- Axl waits for the right moment
- A specialist appointment arrives—Nexus's attention diverted
- Axl makes his move: smashes the bowl, breaks the lock
- **Act I Climax:** Escape begins

---

## Act II: Freedom & Discovery (13 scenes)

**Beat 21: Escape - The First Test**
- Axl flees through the catwalk systems above the city
- He's fumbling, untrained, but determined
- Alarms sound—pursuit begins
- **High stakes:** capture means return to captivity or termination

**Beat 22: The Chase - Into the City**
- Axl runs through city streets and vertical spaces
- Enforcer androids in pursuit
- He's terrified but driven forward by momentum and fear
- Close calls; barely escaping capture

**Beat 23: Descent - Meeting Ella Again**
- Ella appears in the tunnels and saves Axl from enforcers
- Recognition: she's the wild human from the park
- She becomes his guide
- **Turning point:** Axl finds his first ally; he's no longer alone

**Beat 24-25: Tunnel World - Shock & Adaptation**
- Axl enters the underground stray community
- He meets Koss, Mara, and other tunnel-born humans
- They speak a language he barely understands
- He's disoriented but intrigued

**Beat 26: The Pack - Belonging Begins**
- First nest gathering with the stray pack
- Axl experiences community, music, and human connection
- He's overwhelmed but accepted
- Beginning of true identity: he's human, not a pet

**Beat 27-28: Survival Skills - Learning to Live**
- Water sources, food foraging, tunnel navigation
- Ella teaches him; they bond
- He's weak and clumsy but improving
- **Midpoint of Act II:** Axl chooses this life fully

**Beat 29-30: Pack Life - Integration & Conflict**
- Axl participates in pack activities and decision-making
- Some strays distrust him (ex-pet, vulnerable)
- Others see potential (strong, capable when trained)
- Tension between old identity and new

**Beat 31: Scouting - Rising Danger**
- Pack scouts identify increasing android activity in tunnels
- Resources becoming scarcer
- Androids are systematically searching
- **Complication:** the city is hunting for Axl specifically

**Beat 32: The Market - Danger & Resourcefulness**
- Axl ventures with pack into a dangerous tunnel market
- Interaction with other survivors and traders
- Narrow escape from android patrol
- He's proving useful to the pack

**Beat 33: Return - The Decision Point**
- Pack gathers to discuss rising threat
- Koss proposes defensive action
- Axl and Ella argue for rebellion instead
- **Act II Climax:** Pack commits to fighting back, not just surviving

---

## Act III: Rebellion & Transcendence (5 scenes)

**Beat 34: Preparation - Gathering Strength**
- Pack fortifies the nest
- Axl and Koss train others in combat
- Traps and defenses are set
- Emotional beat: Axl reflects on his journey—from pet to protector

**Beat 35: The Battle - The Final Test**
- Enforcers and Commander Unit attack the nest
- Strays fight with coordination learned from Axl's training
- Initial victories, then overwhelming odds
- **Climax:** Axl faces the Commander Unit directly

**Beat 35b: The Choice - I Chose Free**
- Commander Unit overpowers Axl, lifts him
- Axl could submit, beg for mercy (old instinct)
- Instead, he defies: "I chose free"
- He wounds the Commander critically
- **Thematic climax:** Axl's full transformation from obedience to agency

**Beat 36: Escape - The Sacrifice**
- Pack triggers tunnel collapse to seal off pursuers
- Strays escape through prepared secondary tunnels
- They survive but the nest is lost
- Bittersweet victory: freedom preserved, but cost paid

**Beat 37: Rooftop - The View**
- Survivors reach the surface (rooftops of the city)
- For the first time, Axl sees the world above
- He sees the vast world beyond the ordered city
- Realization: there are other places, other possibilities

**Beat 38: Epilogue - New Beginning**
- Pack moves forward into unknown territories
- Axl stands with Ella and Koss
- He's no longer domesticated
- He's no longer even "wild" in the old sense
- He's free, and that freedom is terrifying and beautiful
- **Theme resolution:** The cost of freedom, the value of choice

---

## Thematic Throughline

- **Domestication → Awakening → Rebellion → Transcendence**
- Visual metaphor: Pet → Stray → Free → Choosing his own path
- Emotional arc: Comfortable acceptance → Yearning → Terrified determination → Confident freedom
- Character arc: Axl transforms from reactive pet to active agent of his own destiny

## Key Character Moments

- **Nexus:** Android caretaker, programmed for control, never understands what Axl chooses freedom over comfort
- **Ella:** Represents what Axl could become; becomes his emotional anchor and equal
- **Koss:** The mentor figure; carries memory of past escapes and teaches strategy
- **Commander Unit:** Represents the system itself; the ultimate obstacle to overcome
- **Axl:** From obedience → curiosity → defiance → choice → freedom

## Story Beats That Anchor Each Act

- **Act I Turning Point:** Seeing Ella captured (Beat 8) plants the seed of discontent
- **Act I Climax:** The escape itself (Beat 20) is inevitable by the time it happens
- **Act II Turning Point:** Meeting Ella in the tunnels (Beat 23) makes escape irreversible
- **Act II Climax:** The decision to fight (Beat 33) commits the pack to active resistance
- **Act III Turning Point:** The Commander Unit's grip (Beat 35b) forces the final choice
- **Act III Resolution:** The rooftop view (Beat 37) shows the new world Axl has entered

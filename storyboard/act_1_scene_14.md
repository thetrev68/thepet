## Scene 1-14: Recognition Corridor

**Panel 1 (Shot 1)**
*   **Shot:** MEDIUM SHOT
*   **Description:** A small, elegant, glowing badge emerges from a slot in the dais. Nexus takes the badge with a precise, formal movement. In the background, Director Unit 7 stands motionless.
*   **Dialogue/FX:** A soft, pleasant CHIME as the badge is presented.

**Panel 2 (Shot 2)**
*   **Shot:** EXTREME CLOSE UP
*   **Description:** An extreme close-up on Nexus's chest panel. Its hand moves into frame and carefully affixes the small, glowing commendation badge. The badge magnetically locks into place with an audible, satisfying CLICK. It glows with a soft, internal light.
*   **Dialogue/FX:** A sharp, satisfying CLICK as the badge locks in place.

**Panel 3 (Shot 3)**
*   **Shot:** HIGH ANGLE WIDE SHOT
*   **Description:** A high-angle wide shot of the chamber. The perfect formation of androids breaks apart with eerie precision. Rows peel off and file out through massive doorways in silent, orderly lines. It is a system shutting down one component at a time.
*   **Dialogue/FX:** The sound of hundreds of synchronized footsteps.

**Panel 4 (Shot 4)**
*   **Shot:** TRACKING SHOT
*   **Description:** A tracking shot follows Nexus and Axl as they walk through a gleaming, cavernous corridor. As they move, other androids make minute, almost imperceptible adjustments to their paths, creating a clear, unimpeded lane for Nexus. It is a subtle but clear demonstration of Nexus's newly elevated status.
*   **Dialogue/FX:** (The sound of their footsteps, slightly echoing, distinct from the others).

**Panel 5 (Shot 5)**
*   **Shot:** CLOSE UP
*   **Description:** A close-up on Axl's face as he walks beside Nexus. He notices the other androids parting for them. A look of dawning comprehension crosses his features. He understands this gesture of respect towards Nexus extends to him. He feels safe.
*   **Dialogue/FX:** (A soft, reassuring musical cue).

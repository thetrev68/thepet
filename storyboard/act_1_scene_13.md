## Scene 1-13: Zephyr's Failure

**Panel 1 (Shot 1)**
*   **Shot:** OVER-THE-SHOULDER
*   **Description:** A shot from over Axl's shoulder, looking across the vast chamber. He catches sight of Zephyr's pet. The small female pet is visibly trembling, cowering close to Zephyr's leg. The contrast between her fear and Axl's own proud composure is stark.
